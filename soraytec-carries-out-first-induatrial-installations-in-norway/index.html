Soraytec Carries Out First Induatrial Installations in Norway

After successful type testing the Soraytec IoT Sensor for sub zero temperatures and receiving the go ahead for installation from our Norwegian partner. Soraytec is happy to inform that after long and meticulous testing of the technology during sub zero temperatures and high humidity levels two devices are operational in Norway as part of an industrial pilot. Our partner is keen to further analyze the technology and to install more devices in the 24kV network in order to monitor critical intersections, once Soraytec’s 24kV device is fully type tested for the Norwegian market. Soraytec expects the first field deployments from its 24kV series to start in 2024.
